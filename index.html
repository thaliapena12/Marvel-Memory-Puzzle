Marvel Memory Puzzle

Instructions:


Player turns over any two cards (one at a time), if they match the cards will be removed. When player turns over two cards that do not match, those cards are turned face down again (in the same position) and they get to try again untilthe board is clear. The trick is to remember which cards are where.
